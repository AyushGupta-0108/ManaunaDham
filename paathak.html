About Paathak Complex

Paathak Complex is located on Aonla-Bisauli Road, just 300 metres from the main gate of Manauna Dham — making it one of the closest accommodations to Khatu Shyam Ji temple. Walk to the temple in just 5 minutes, morning or evening, without needing an auto or taxi.

Whether you are visiting for darshan, a festival, or an extended stay, Paathak Complex offers clean, comfortable rooms at honest prices. Choose the cool AC Room for a comfortable summer stay, or the spacious Non-AC Grand Room with two double beds — ideal for families and pilgrim groups travelling together.

A very spacious parking area makes it stress-free for guests arriving by car or bus, and nearby shops and dhabas ensure food is always within easy reach.
