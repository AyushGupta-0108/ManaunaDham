How to Reach Manauna Dham

Manauna Dham (near Aonla, Bareilly) is well connected by road, rail and air. Our pilgrim help desk can arrange transport from all major hubs.

Taxi / Cab

Direct cabs from Bareilly, Aonla, Bareilly Airport or Railway Station. Best for families and elderly pilgrims.

Book Taxi

Bus

Buses run from Bareilly to Aonla. From Aonla Bus Stand, auto or taxi will take you to Manauna Dham.

Book Bus

Train

Nearest stations are Bareilly Junction and Aonla Railway Station. Local transport available from both.

Book Train

Auto / Rickshaw

Autos are available from Aonla Bus Stand and Railway Station for the final short ride to the Dham.

Book Auto

Approximate Distances

  • Aonla: Very close ~2 km (local ride required)
  • Bareilly: 43–53 km
  • Delhi: 250–300 km
  • Jaipur: 400+ km

Bus Travel

Electric buses run from Bareilly (Chaupala Chauraha) to Manauna Dham from 6 AM to 5 PM (fare ~₹100). Regular UP Roadways buses go to Aonla, from where autos & taxis take you to the temple.

Railway

Bareilly Junction is the major hub. Aonla Railway Station is closer for local travel. Taxis & autos available from both.

Air Travel

Bareilly Airport is the closest (53 km). Delhi and Jaipur are major connecting airports with better flight options.
