Train se Aane Wale Bhakt

Bareilly Junction sabse bada railway station hai. Aonla Railway Station sabse kareeb hai Manauna Dham se. Dono jagah se taxi aur auto mil jaati hai — hum arrange kar sakte hain.

Flight se Aane Wale Bhakt

Bareilly Airport sabse kareeb hai (53 km). Delhi aur Jaipur se bhi flights milti hain — wahan se cab book karwa sakte hain Manauna Dham tak.

Apni Gaadi se Aayein

Apni gaadi se aane wale bhakton ke liye — Aonla-Bareilly highway pe Manauna Dham clearly sign-posted hai. Parking ki suvidha available hai Dham ke paas.
