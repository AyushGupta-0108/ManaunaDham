Manauna (Manona) Dham

Manauna Dham, also known as Shree Khatu Shyam Ji Manauna Dham, is one of the most rapidly growing spiritual pilgrimage sites in India. Located near Aonla in Bareilly district, the Dham attracts lakhs of devotees every day seeking blessings, peace, and relief from life's difficulties.

Devotees believe that sincere prayer here brings divine grace through Shri Khatu Shyam Ji. The temple is especially known for its sacred rituals conducted by its Chief Priest.

✍️ Manauna is the correct spelling — Manona is the common regional variant.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Manauna Dham located?

Manauna Dham is located in Manauna village near Aonla, Bareilly district, Uttar Pradesh – 243301.

What are the darshan timings?

Darshan is available from 7 AM to 9 PM daily. Aarti is performed at 8 AM and 5 PM.

How to get a patient token?

Patient tokens can be given only when patients are present in Manauna Dham. We do not issue tokens ourselves — this is based on prevailing on-site practices and may change.

Is this the official temple website?

No. ManaunaDham.org.in is a privately operated pilgrim information and services platform. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by the temple management.

How to reach Manauna Dham from Delhi?

Take a train from Delhi to Aonla (AWL) station, then auto/taxi to Manauna village (~10 km). You can check trains and book local transport using our services above.
