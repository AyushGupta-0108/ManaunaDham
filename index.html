About Manauna Dham

Manauna Dham is a rapidly growing spiritual destination located in Bareilly district of Uttar Pradesh. Dedicated to Shri Khatu Shyam Ji, this dham attracts thousands of devotees who come to seek blessings, attend aarti and participate in spiritual activities.

Inspired by faith and devotion, Manauna Dham has emerged as an important pilgrimage centre in North India, similar to how Bageshwar Dham grew through spiritual belief and social media.
