Manauna Dham

Manauna Dham, also known as Shree Khatu Shyam Ji Manauna Dham, is a famous faith-based healing pilgrimage site near Aonla, Bareilly. Devotees believe blessings, holy water and sacred rituals bring peace and healing.

Mandir Timings

Darshan: 7 AM – 9 PM

Aarti: 8 AM & 5 PM

Best Time: June – December

Mahant Ji unavailable Wed & Thu
