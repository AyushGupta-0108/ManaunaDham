About Manauna Dham

Manauna Dham, also referred to in online sources as Manauna / Manona Khatu Shyam Ji Temple, is a growing pilgrimage site near Aonla in Bareilly district, Uttar Pradesh. Devotees visit the shrine for darshan and worship of Khatu Shyam Ji. It has become increasingly popular with visitors sharing experiences online and in local travel guides.

While the temple’s official timings and details are being confirmed, many pilgrims report large gatherings during weekends and festivals. This site will provide verified details as soon as they are available.

How to Reach

Manauna Dham is located in the Bareilly district of Uttar Pradesh with road connections from nearby cities. The nearest major city is Bareilly, and local transportation (bus, taxi) is common from Aonla.
