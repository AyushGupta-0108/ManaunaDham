Manauna(Manona) Dham

Manauna Dham, also known as Shree Khatu Shyam Ji Manauna Dham, is one of the most rapidly growing spiritual pilgrimage sites in india. Located near Aonla in Bareilly district, the Dham attracts lakhs of devotees every day who come seeking blessings, peace, and relief from life's difficulties.

Devotees believe that sincere prayer at Manauna Dham brings divine grace through Shri Khatu Shyam Ji. The temple is especially known for its sacred rituals conducted by it's Chief Priest(Mahant Shri Omendra Chauhan).

People travel here from across all over India and abroad to participate in darshan, aarti, and to seek blessings of Shri Khatu Shyam Ji.

Manauna is the correct spelling. Manona is the common variant.

Frequently Asked Questions about Manauna (Manona) Dham

Where is Manauna Dham located?
Manauna Dham is located in Manauna village near Aonla in Bareilly district, Uttar Pradesh.

What are the darshan timings?
Darshan is available from 7 AM to 9 PM daily. Aarti is performed at 8 AM and 5 PM.

How to get a patient token?
Patient tokens can be given to patients only who are in manauna dham. Rest all should take normal line for abhimantrit jal and darshan.

Is this the official temple website?
No. ManaunaDham.org.in is a privately operated pilgrim information and services platform.
