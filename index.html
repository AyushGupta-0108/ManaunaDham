Manauna Dham

Manauna Dham, also known as Shree Khatu Shyam Ji Manauna Dham, is a popular Hindu pilgrimage site near Aonla (Bareilly), famous for its strong faith-based healing. Devotees believe miraculous cures for illnesses, even critical ones, occur through rituals such as tying threads on a sacred Siddh tree and drinking holy water blessed by the chief priest, Mahant Omendra Chauhan. Large crowds and even ambulances with seriously ill patients regularly arrive seeking divine intervention, peace, and blessings.

Mandir Timings

Darshan: 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Aarti: 8:00 AM & 5:00 PM

Best Time to Visit: June to December (pleasant weather)

Crowds: Heavy – plan extra time or full-day visit

Mahant Ji Availability: Usually not available on Wednesdays & Thursdays (Darshan remains open)

Festivals: Timings may vary during special occasions
