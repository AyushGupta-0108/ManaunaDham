Manauna Dham

Manauna Dham, also known as Shree Khatu Shyam Ji Manauna Dham, is one of the most rapidly growing spiritual pilgrimage sites in india. Located near Aonla in Bareilly district, the Dham attracts lakhs of devotees every day who come seeking blessings, peace, and relief from life’s difficulties.

Devotees believe that sincere prayer at Manauna Dham brings divine grace through Shri Khatu Shyam Ji. The temple is especially known for its strong faith-based traditions, spiritual atmosphere, and the sacred rituals conducted under the guidance of the Mahant.

People travel here from across all over India and abroad to participate in darshan, aarti, and special prayer ceremonies.

Mandir Timings

Darshan: 7 AM – 9 PM

Aarti: 8 AM & 5 PM

Best Time: June – December

Mahant Ji unavailable: Wednesday & Thursday
