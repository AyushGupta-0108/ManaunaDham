Manauna Dham

Manauna Dham, also known as Shree Khatu Shyam Ji Manauna Dham, is a popular Hindu pilgrimage site near Aonla (Bareilly), famous for its strong faith-based healing where devotees believe miraculous cures for illnesses occur through rituals such as tying threads on a Siddh tree and drinking holy water blessed by Mahant Omendra Chauhan.

Mandir Timings

Darshan: 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Aarti: 8:00 AM & 5:00 PM

Best Time: June – December

Mahant Ji: Not available Wed & Thu

Festivals: Timings may vary
