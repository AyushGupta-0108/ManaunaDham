About Hotel Raj Garden

Hotel Raj Garden is located on Temple Road, just 3–4 km from Manauna Dham (Manona Dham) — making it one of the closest hotels to Khatu Shyam Ji temple in Aonla. Whether you are visiting for darshan, a festival, or an extended stay, Raj Garden is your ideal pilgrim base.

Deluxe rooms offer a beautiful park view with balcony access — perfect for a peaceful morning before heading to the temple. The in-house restaurant means you never have to worry about finding food.
