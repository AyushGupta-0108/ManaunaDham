Mahant Shri Omendra Chauhan

Mahant Shri Omendra Singh Chauhan is the spiritual head of Manauna Dham in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh. Born in 1984 in Manona village, he transformed from a national social activist into a spiritual healer devoted to Shri Khatu Shyam Ji.

He founded Manauna Dham in 2021 after a spiritual awakening and today guides thousands of devotees seeking healing, peace and divine blessings.

Mahant Ji is known for the sacred “SHYAM JAL” (blessed water) prepared through mantra chanting, which devotees believe brings relief from illness and suffering.

Spiritual Work & Dham

Manauna Dham spans over 20 bighas and serves thousands of pilgrims every week. The dham offers free water, dharamshala accommodation and spiritual counseling to devotees from across India.
