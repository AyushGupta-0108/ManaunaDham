Mahant Omendra Chauhan



Mahant Shri Omendra Chauhan is the spiritual head of Shri Khatu Shyam Ji Manauna Dham, located in Manona village, Aonla Tehsil, Bareilly District, Uttar Pradesh. He is known for his devotional leadership and for attracting large numbers of pilgrims who visit the Dham seeking peace, blessings and spiritual comfort.

Born in 1984, Mahant Ji’s life journey includes a period of social activism before turning fully toward spiritual life. In 2021, he dedicated himself to establishing Manauna Dham as a center for bhakti (devotion), prayer, and faith-based spiritual support for devotees of Shri Khatu Shyam Ji.

Under his leadership, Manauna Dham has grown into a major pilgrimage site. Thousands of devotees visit the temple every month from across Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Delhi NCR and other regions.

A distinctive aspect of Manauna Dham is the practice of distributing sanctified water, commonly called “Shyam Jal”. Devotees believe this water, blessed through prayer and mantra, brings spiritual strength, hope, and relief from suffering. Many visitors come with deep faith, seeking divine grace for physical, emotional, and family problems.

“I am not a miracle-worker. I am only a medium. All blessings come from Shri Shyam Baba.” – Mahant Omendra Chauhan

Manauna Dham operates as a community-focused spiritual center. Free or low-cost dharamshala accommodation is available for pilgrims, and major Hindu festivals such as Holi, Janmashtami, and the annual Phalgun Mela are celebrated with large gatherings and devotional programs.

Mahant Ji also maintains an active digital presence through social media and video platforms, allowing devotees who cannot travel to remain connected with the Dham and its spiritual activities.

Note: Manauna Dham is a faith-based spiritual institution. Devotional practices and spiritual healing are matters of personal belief. Visitors are encouraged to continue appropriate medical and professional care alongside spiritual activities.
