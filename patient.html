‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Kya patient token advance book kar sakte hain?

A: Nahi. Patient token advance book nahi hota. Patient ko mandir mein aakar hi line mein lagna hota hai.

Q: Mahant Ji Wednesday-Thursday kyun nahi baithte?

A: Yeh unke regular off days hain. Inn dino patient token system available nahi hota.

Q: Agar main Wednesday ya Thursday aa jaun?

A: Aap general Shyam Jal line mein lag sakte hain. Lekin Mahant Ji ka personal darshan nahi hoga.

Q: Hotel booking ke liye advance payment chahiye?

A: Haan, booking confirm karne ke liye advance payment liya jaata hai. Details hotel page par available hain.

Q: Food delivery mandir ke andar milta hai?

A: Nahi. Food delivery aapke hotel/dharamshala ya mandir ke bahar designated area mein milti hai.
