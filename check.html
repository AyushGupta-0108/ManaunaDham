About Manauna Dham

Shree Khatu Shyam Ji Manauna Dham is a revered shrine in Manauna village near Aonla, Bareilly district, Uttar Pradesh. Devotees from across India visit to seek the blessings of Shyam Baba — the Hari of Kalyug.

ManaunaDham.org.in is a privately operated pilgrim platform that helps you plan your darshan — booking hotels, food, taxi, Shyam Jal and more — so your journey is comfortable and peaceful.

Frequently Asked Questions

About Manauna (Manona) Dham

Where is Manauna Dham located?▼

Manauna Dham is located in Manauna village near Aonla in Bareilly district, Uttar Pradesh.

What are the darshan timings?▼

Darshan is available from 5 AM to 9 PM daily (with a midday break). Aarti is performed in morning and evening.

How to get a patient token?▼

Patient tokens can be given only when patients are present in Manauna Dham. We do not issue patient tokens. This information is based on prevailing on-site practices and may change.

Is this the official temple website?▼

No. ManaunaDham.org.in is a privately operated pilgrim information and services platform.
