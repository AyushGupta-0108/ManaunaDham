Manauna (Manona) Dham

Manauna Dham, also called Manona Dham, is the sacred pilgrimage site of Shri Khatu Shyam Ji in Bareilly district.

Manauna is the correct spelling. Manona is the common variant.

Manauna Dham Bareilly – Address & Aarti

Address: Manauna Village, Aonla, Bareilly, UP – 243301

Aarti: 8 AM & 5 PM
