Manauna Dham

Manauna Dham, also known as Shree Khatu Shyam Ji Manauna Dham, is a prominent spiritual pilgrimage site near Aonla in Bareilly district, Uttar Pradesh.

Devotees visit Manauna Dham for darshan, aarti, and spiritual peace.

Mandir Timings

Darshan: 7 AM – 9 PM

Aarti: 8 AM & 5 PM

Best Time: June – December

Mahant Ji unavailable: Wednesday & Thursday
